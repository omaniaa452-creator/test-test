# Pricing Packages (Tunisia)

## Starter (Recommended)
- Price: 250–300 TND
- Up to 12 products
- 1 preset + branding
- WhatsApp ordering (Messenger optional)

## Standard
- Price: 320–450 TND
- Up to 20 products
- Featured + manual order
- Better copy + trust blocks

## Premium
- Price: 500–700 TND
- Up to 35 products
- Extra copywriting + niche tuning
- 14 days support

Notes:
- Avoid “too cheap” offers (support becomes painful).
- Always show a demo before quoting final price.
