# Seller Guide (V11.2)

This product is a **mobile-first storefront** that sells via **WhatsApp + Messenger** (no checkout, no dashboard).
Your job is not to “sell a website”. Your job is to **increase orders** and **reduce friction** for a Facebook page.

## What you are selling (simple)
- A fast product catalog (works great on Android)
- A product details sheet (feels like an app)
- One-tap ordering via WhatsApp/Messenger
- Easy customization via config + presets (no coding per client)

## Best offer structure (Tunisia)
### Offer A — Entry (best for first clients)
- Up to **12 products**
- Preset + colors + copy
- WhatsApp orders (Messenger optional)
- Delivery: 24–48h

### Offer B — Standard
- Up to **20 products**
- Featured section + manual order
- PWA install instructions (if enabled later)
- Delivery: 48–72h

### Offer C — Premium (only when requested)
- Up to **35 products**
- Extra copywriting + trust blocks tuning
- 14 days support

## How to pitch (copy you can paste)
**Message (short):**
> سلام! شفت صفحتكم… نقدر نحولها لواجهة طلب مرتبة على الهاتف: زبون يشوف المنتجات، يفتح التفاصيل، ويطلب في واتساب/ميسنجر في أقل من دقيقة. تحب نوريك ديمو؟

**If they ask “price?”**
> حسب عدد المنتجات والتخصيص. عادة بين 250 و350 د. لو تحب نبدأ بـ 12 منتج ونطلعوا بأفضل نسخة للهاتف.

## What to show in demo (2 minutes)
1) Home → products grid
2) Tap a product → Product Sheet
3) Tap WhatsApp → message is ready
4) Show “Featured” / sorting (if enabled)
5) Show how fast it is

## Don’t do this
- Don’t mention React/Vite.
- Don’t promise “SEO / Google ranking” unless you actually provide it.
- Don’t build dashboards (support nightmare).

## Upsells that actually sell
- Extra products pack (+10 products)
- Copy improvement (hero + trust)
- Custom preset for their niche
- PWA install (later)
